Who we are

In a world driven by opportunities, passion, dedication and facts stabilized by the same, you cannot afford to ignore the critical advantages of serving young people with quality availabe mentorship. This is what births the why for every growing up teen towards self discovery. Other than hardwork, no one can play it right without a substantial personal commitment in utilizing readily available opportunities. After observing the fundamental importance of mentorship play in one's growth, we founded Alumi Mentors to help youngsters unveil their talents and potential.

What we do

Here we believe in hard work, creativity, and integrity. We aim to continue mentorship and life skills bootcamp programs to students by equipping them with basic coding skills, life skills and in areas of academics. We beleive that the future of technology is bright in Kenya.
